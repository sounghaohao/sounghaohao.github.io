About me

I am a PhD student in the System and Networking Group (SNG), led by Prof. Jiwu Shu and Prof. Qiao Xiang. My research interests focus on communication-efficient federated learning that optimizes communication patterns for wall-clock time and client sampling for heterogeneity.
